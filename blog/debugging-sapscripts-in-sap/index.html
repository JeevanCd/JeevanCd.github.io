Debugging SAPscripts in SAP

written by Jeevan Chand on 2019-10-06

To activate SAP Script debugger:

Step 1:

Go to Transaction SE38.
Enter the program name RSTXDBUG


Press Execute.

Step 2:

Go to SE71.

In the initial screen, Enter the form name and click on Utilities --> Activate Debugger
